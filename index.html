Who We Are

Institutional Compliance and Governance Infrastructure.

GAIATECH is not a software company. We operate as an institutional ecosystem designed to organize information, validate processes and connect governments, organizations and initiatives under clear governance and international standards.

Our work focuses on building environments of trust, regulatory alignment and strategic articulation — especially in areas such as diplomatic cooperation, ESG frameworks, public innovation and global institutional integration.

Technology is only a means, never the end. What defines GAIATECH is the creation of structured models that transform complex ideas into viable, credible and internationally aligned initiatives.

By acting as an intelligent compliance infrastructure, GAIATECH helps institutions navigate regulatory challenges, strengthen governance practices and establish bridges between Brazil and the global ecosystem.

Institutional Leadership

GAIATECH was founded by Reinaldo Adeodato, an institutional strategist focused on governance design, international articulation and the development of structured initiatives capable of connecting innovation, public systems and global cooperation.
